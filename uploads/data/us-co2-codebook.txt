Total CO2 emissions from fossil-fuels (1000 metric tons) for the United States

Data source:
  Source organization(s):      CDIAC (Carbon Dioxide Information Analysis Center)	
  Link to source organization: http://cdiac.ornl.gov/	
  File name:                   nation.1751_2009.csv	
  Link to complete reference:  http://cdiac.ornl.gov/ftp/ndp030/CSV-FILES/
  
Data attributes:
  year:
  total_c:     Total CO2 emissions from fossil-fuels (thousand metric tons of C)
  solid:       Emissions from solid fuel consumption
  liquid:      Emissions from liquid fuel consumption
  gas:         Emissions from gas fuel consumption
  cement:      Emissions from cement production
  gas_flare:   Emissions from gas flaring
  per_capita:  Per capita CO2 emissions (metric tons of carbon)
  bunker:      Emissions from bunker fuels (not included in the totals)
  
Notes:
  To convert original data from amount of Carbon (thousand metric tons of C) 
  into Carbon Dioxide (CO2), use the following formula: C*[(12+16*2)/12]
